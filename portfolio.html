The Carpinteria warriors faced Cate rams in early January of Bailor's senior year in a trilling contest. The rivalry between the two high school in the small town of Carpinteria, CA. goes way back to the mid 19th century. Cate started this match on the wrong foot as they let the warriors get ahead in the first 20min. The rams hadn't play a game in 47days but that was no excuse. They battle for the remainder of the game but the warriors were in a much better position and were able to secure a W.

In this gamne against Foothill Tech High School, another major Cate rival, the rams weere able to turn things around and come up with a good result.
